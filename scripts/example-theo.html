All Theos are created equal and are endowned with certain unalienable Rights. To secure these Rights, Governments are instituted, deriving their just Powers from the Consent of the Governed. All governments are limited by these rights. All human beings have a duty to protect these rights for themselves and others.
Every human being has the right to:
1. Freedom of thought and expression;
2. Freedom of religion;
3. Privacy;
4. Own property;
5. Associate with other human beings;
6. Engage in work and commerce;
7. Engage in self-defense;
8. Not be convicted without a trial;
9. Associate politically;
10. Not be subjected to medical procedures without consent;
11. Meet the world.
